Buckskin Youth Leadership Training Course

LEARN

Our scouts learn 13 fundamental leadership skills for starting or continuing their progress as youth leaders in a troop and in their community. They also learn various beginner and advanced outdoor skills and techniques to extend the standard scouting curriculum. Click here to learn more about these skills.

PRACTICE

Buckskin gives each scout the opportunity to practice each leadership, scouting, and soft skill in a safe and structured environment with the guidance of an experienced all-Eagle Scout staff with over 35 years of scout educational experience. Click here to learn more about how our scouts practice what they learn.

IMPLEMENT

For every 1 hour of classroom-style learning and practice, Buckskin scouts will experience at least 2 hours of skill implementation in an outdoor environment. With over 70 targeted games and 3 major challenges, these scouts will not only walk away with fundamental knowledge but also hands-on experience. Click here to learn more about the Buckskin Experience.
